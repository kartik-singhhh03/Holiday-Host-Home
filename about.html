Our Story

Born from a Love of
Luxury & Hospitality

Holiday Home Host began with a simple vision: to bring true 5-star hospitality to the holiday rental market in Ras Al Khaimah. We saw a gap between the experience guests deserved and what they were actually receiving — and we built our company to close it.

Founded on Hayat Island, Mina Al Arab, our properties sit at the heart of RAK's most prestigious waterfront address. Every apartment in our portfolio has been personally curated, designed, and prepared to deliver the kind of stay our guests tell their friends about.

Today, we manage a growing collection of luxury sea-view apartments, serve hundreds of guests from across the world each year, and are proud to partner with discerning property owners who share our commitment to excellence.
